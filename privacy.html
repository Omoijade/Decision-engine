Privacy

Decision Engine can run entirely in your browser. By default it does not require an account and it does not send your text to a server.

Local storage

Drafts are saved in your browser using localStorage. This keeps your work on your device.

Sharing

The tool generates text you can copy into email. What happens next depends on where you paste it (your email system, recipients, and your organization’s policies).

Export

You can export a decision as JSON. Where you store exported files is up to you and your organization.

Sensitive information

Avoid putting sensitive personal information into the tool. If you need to store sensitive data, use systems approved by your organization.

Analytics

This demo does not require analytics to function. If you add analytics later, do not collect decision content.
