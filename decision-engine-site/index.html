01

How it works

Step 1
Draft the decision

Title + one-sentence summary. Add reasoning and a revisit date if it’s uncertain.

Step 2
Assign next steps

Write steps starting with an owner (e.g., “Alex: …”). The preview highlights owners for readability.

Step 3
Copy the message

Generate an email-ready message and paste it into your email client.

02

Privacy & safety

  • Local-first: by default, your draft is stored only in your browser (localStorage).
  • No accounts: no login, no user tracking required to use the tool.
  • Data hygiene: avoid sensitive personal data in decision drafts. Treat this as an operational drafting aid.
  • Portability: you can export JSON to store decisions in your own systems.
