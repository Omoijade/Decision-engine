Privacy

Decision Engine is designed to be lightweight and local-first.

What we store

By default, drafts are saved in your browser using localStorage. If you clear your browser storage, drafts may be removed.

What you should not include

Do not include sensitive personal data (e.g., identifiers, medical details, financial details). Treat this as an operational drafting tool.

Export

You can export decision data as JSON. Where you store exported files is up to you and your organization.

Analytics

This demo site does not require analytics to function. If you add analytics later, avoid collecting decision content.
